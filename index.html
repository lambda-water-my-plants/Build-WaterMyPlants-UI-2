Learn More

Life is hectic enough without trying to remember the watering schedules for each member of your indoor garden. We can help! When you create a profile, you can add each of your plants and set an individual watering schedule to meet each plant's individual watering needs. You set the schedule but we'll keep track of it for you and keep you on track with text message reminders for each plant in your home.
